Baluarte Zoo

Baluarte is a zoo in Vigan City, Ilocos Sur, Philippines, owned by Chavit Singson. It was established in 2011 and spans 80 hectares. It houses different animals endemic to the Philippines and some from other countries, offering an interactive and educational experience for all ages.

Calle Crisologo

Calle Crisologo is a major street in the city of Vigan, Ilocos Sur, Philippines. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is known for its cobblestone streets and old Spanish houses. The street is a popular tourist destination and is home to many shops and restaurants, offering a glimpse into the colonial past.

Bantay Church

The Saint Augustine Parish Church, commonly known as the Bantay Church, is a Roman Catholic church in Bantay, Ilocos Sur. Established in 1590, it is one of the oldest churches in the Philippines. Adjacent to the church is the Bantay Bell Tower, which served as a watchtower during World War II, providing panoramic views.

Syquia Mansion

The Syquia Mansion (also known as the Quirino Mansion) is one of the most visited heritage houses in Vigan City. It is the ancestral home of the former President Elpidio Quirino. The house is well-preserved and is a great example of Spanish colonial architecture, filled with antique furniture and historical artifacts.

#syquiamansion #vigan #quirino #heritagehouse #history #architecture

Inabel Fabric

Inabel is a handwoven fabric made by the Ilocano people of the Philippines. It is made from cotton and is known for its intricate designs, often featuring geometric patterns and scenes from daily life. The fabric is used to make clothing, blankets, and other items, showcasing the rich textile tradition of the region.
